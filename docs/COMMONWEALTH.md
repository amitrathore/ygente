# Ygente Positioning Note

## Status

The previous "Network Commonwealth" positioning has been retired because it
created confusion about what Ygente is.

Ygente should now be described as a **founder-led growth network** for Hispanic
commerce. Ygente owns and operates the platform, protects the brand, sets
standards, and enters into normal legal agreements with participants.

## Current Public Model

The public story should be:

- Ygente is led by founders and executives.
- Ambassadors and creators earn from trusted product recommendations.
- Brands reach Hispanic consumers through authentic community voices.
- Regional partners may help develop local markets under Ygente agreements.
- Market structure can remain geographically organized, but it is a Ygente
  operating model, not self-governance.
- Participation is governed by contracts, platform terms, licenses, policies,
  and commercial programs.

## Terms to Use

Use:

- Ygente
- Founder-led
- Executive team
- Regional partner
- Market
- Ygente principles
- Ygente agreements
- Market development

Avoid in public copy:

- Commonwealth
- Constitution
- Steward / stewardship
- Territory
- Subsidiarity
- Council
- Community treasury
- Royalty
- Covenant
- Self-governing communities

## Implementation Note

The current app still keeps the existing `/commonwealth/...` route paths and
translation key names to avoid unnecessary routing churn. Those identifiers are
implementation details for now; public-facing copy should use the Ygente-first
positioning above.
